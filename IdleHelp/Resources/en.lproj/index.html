Forest Link Function

The Forest Account Link function allows you to synchronize your data with Forest on your phone. The synchronized data includes the following:

  • Focused clock successes (countdown ends and timing stops)
  • Failure of the clock (clicked the "Stop" button during the countdown)
  • Focus Clock Tags

    • The synchronization process will be automatic and the data will be synchronized at the end of each focus. If there is a temporary problem with synchronization, up to 5 synchronized focus records can be temporarily saved.

    【Note I】 Synchronized Focus Clocks are recorded between 15 ~ 180 minutes and in multiples of 5. For example, a 24-minute concentration will be synchronized to 20 minutes, while a 10-minute concentration will not be synchronized.

    【Note II】 If Forest Account synchronization is turned on, you will not be able to edit the tag list for the Focus Clock on this Mac. To edit it, please edit it in Forest on your phone and then do a sync.

    【Note III】 The amount of focus time in this application is not part of the "Deep Focus" time in Forest. You can view the focus records in the Forests tab, but the records will not be recorded in the Focus Leaderboard.
